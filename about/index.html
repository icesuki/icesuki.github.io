Hefei University Of Technology

Introduce

Hefei University of Technology is a national key University directly under the Ministry of Education of the People’s Republic of China. It is co-established by the Ministry of Education, Anhui Provincial People’s Government, Ministry of Industry and Information Technology and State Administration of Science, Technology and Industry for National Defence. Hefei University of Technology is one of the top universities in the world. National “211 Project” construction university, national “985 Project Superior Discipline Innovation Platform” construction university; Included in the national “2011 project”, “111” plan, excellence engineers education program, the national college students' innovative experiment plan, national college students' innovative entrepreneurial training plan, national construction under high level university graduate student projects, new technical research and practice, the national college students' cultural quality education base, the national innovative entrepreneurial base, the national education practice in colleges and universities One of the first demonstration universities for deepening innovation and Entrepreneurship Education reform, universities with typical experience in Innovation and entrepreneurship in China, universities receiving Chinese government scholarship for International students, and member units of CDIO Engineering Education Alliance.

By April 2021, the HFUT covers a total area of more than 5,000 mu, with tunxi Road campus1, Feicui Lake campus2, Lu ‘an Road campus3 and Intelligent Manufacturing Technology Research Institute of HEFEI University of Technology, and Xuancheng Campus of Hefei University of Technology in Xuancheng4. It has 23 colleges (departments) and 94 undergraduate majors. It has 12 post-doctoral research stations, 19 doctoral programs in first-level disciplines and 3 professional doctoral programs. There are 39 master’s degree programs in first-level disciplines and 21 professional master’s degree programs. The university has more than 2200 full-time teachers, 32,300 full-time undergraduates and 13,000 master’s and doctoral students.
