“System Engineering Theory and Application of Intelligent Interconnected Systems”, Project of Basic Science Center of National Natural Science Foundation of China, Hefei University of Technology

Date: 2021-12-14 Source: Party and Government Office (Development and Planning Office)

Recently, the basic science Center project of “Systems Engineering Theory and Application of Intelligent Interconnected Systems” was officially launched by the National Natural Science Foundation of China. The project is led and hosted by Hefei University of Technology, with Tsinghua University and Global Energy Internet Research Institute Co., LTD as partners. With a direct funding of 50 million yuan and a five-year funding period, the project is the first approved and hosted basic science center project in Anhui Province.

Basic science center of national natural science fund project is the foundation project is by far the highest to locate funds, aimed at to major national demand and international academic front as the guide, the basic subject issues in centralized related fields of scientific research resources, relying on the high level academic leaders, attract and outstanding scientific and technological personnel, strengthen the basic research, scientific depth of intersection, It is committed to making breakthroughs in the forefront of science, producing original achievements of international leading level, and forming an academic highland with international important influence.

On September 28, 2021, the basic Science Center project “System Engineering Theory and Application of Intelligent Interconnected Systems” passed the field investigation of Natural Science Foundation of China. Foundation center for leadership and expert group listened to the project director of the report, and the project director, project backbone members and hefei university of technology, tsinghua university, global energy research institute co., LTD., head of the unit such as the Internet has carried on the discussion, and examines the supporting institution research condition of hefei university of technology and research work. Zhang Hongwen, Vice Governor of Anhui Province, Wu Jinsong, Deputy Secretary general of Anhui Provincial Government, and relevant leaders of Provincial Science and Technology Department and Provincial Education Department attended the on-site inspection meeting, and held discussions with NSFC on supporting the construction of basic science center project.

After full discussion, the expert group believes that the project team has achieved a number of high-level research results, which provide a solid foundation for the establishment of the Basic Science Center project, facing the major national needs and international academic frontiers. The research direction among project team members is highly complementary and cooperative, and the research conditions and software and hardware environment are good. The project research is original, the research objective is clear, the research scheme is feasible, and it is recommended to start.

The successful approval of the project is an important achievement of our university’s “Double First-class” construction, and will further boost the construction of an internationally renowned research-oriented high-level university and a number of world-class disciplines.
